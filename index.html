I like gacha games

I play a good number of gacha games and spend an ungodly amount of time playing them. Gacha games are typically mobile games that have a feature that allows players to spend in-game resources to have a chance of obtaining characters in that game. If the player runs out of those resources, the games typically offer ways to restock on those resources using real life money. The gacha game that I am primarily playing right now is Arknights
